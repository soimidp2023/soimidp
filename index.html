Real-Time Soil Monitoring System for Oil Palm Plantation

SoiM

Agriculture New and Improved

Real-Time Soil Monitoring (SoiM) for oil palm plantation is a device that can enhance the task efficiency in oil palm plantation in which the device was developed to monitor the soil condition which includes soil moisture, soil pH and ambient temperature with utilization of Internet of Things (IoT) to send data to the Blynk application. SoiM allow user to monitor the condition of soil via data from clouds anywhere and anytime.
